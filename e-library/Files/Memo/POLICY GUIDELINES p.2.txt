<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: 'times new roman', times;">&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: 'times new roman', times;">GENERAL POLICY</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: 'times new roman', times;">&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<ol>
<li><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: 'times new roman', times;">This program covers salaried, pension or other fixed compensation employees/pensioners only. It does not pertain to or refer to general consumer lending as practical in the financial market place.</span></li>
</ol>
</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: 'times new roman', times;">&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<ol start="2">
<li><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: 'times new roman', times;">Credit under this program is of a policy nature. The granting shall refer to unsecured loans for a broad range consumption purposes, granted to individuals mainly on the basis of regular salary, pension or other fixed compensation, where repayment would come from such future cash flows, either through salary deductions, debits from the borrower&rsquo;s deposit account, mobile payments, pay-through collections, over the counter payments or other type of payment arrangement agreed upon by the borrower and lender.</span></li>
</ol>
</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: 'times new roman', times;">&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: 'times new roman', times;">Ability/willingness to pay and profitability guidelines apply.</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: 'times new roman', times;">&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<ol start="3">
<li><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: 'times new roman', times;">One to one (1:1) loans do not fall under this program. Such loans are to be treated under guidelines specifically related to one to one (1:1) transaction.</span></li>
</ol>
</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: 'times new roman', times;">&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<ol start="4">
<li><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: 'times new roman', times;">A borrower will have only one (1) loan outstanding under the program at any one time. Husband and wife are to be considered as single borrower.</span></li>
</ol>
</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: 'times new roman', times;">&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<ol start="5">
<li><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: 'times new roman', times;">The loan must be co-signed jointly and severally by the spouse of the borrower. Where the borrower is single or separated, an acceptable co-maker with at least equal capacity to pay as the borrower must co-make the loan. Where the borrower is married but the spouse is incapacitated, an acceptable co-maker with at least equal capacity to pay as the borrower must co-make the loan. Where the borrower is a widow, a co-maker with at least equal capacity to pay as the borrower preferably relatives must co-make the loan.</span></li>
</ol>
</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: 'times new roman', times;">&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<ol start="6">
<li><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: 'times new roman', times;">Loans to pay-off existing obligations which are past due are prohibited.</span></li>
</ol>
</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: 'times new roman', times;">&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<ol start="7">
<li><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: 'times new roman', times;">Loans for business purposes, such as working capital and/or purchase of equipment for a business enterprise, do not fall under this program. These are to be treated as regular business loans.</span></li>
</ol>
</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: 'times new roman', times;">&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<ol start="8">
<li><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: 'times new roman', times;">Changes in manual provision are to be approved by the Credit Committee who shall report such change approvals to the Board for notation.</span></li>
</ol>
