<div style="text-align: justify;">
<ol>
<li><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: 'times new roman', times;">Granting of this type of loan is consistent with best practices and sound credit processes prescribed under BSP regulations, coupled with comprehensive assessment of the borrower&rsquo;s creditworthiness, and is not purely based on multiples of monthly salary/compensation.</span></li>
</ol>
</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: 'times new roman', times;">&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<ol start="2">
<li><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: 'times new roman', times;">Individual borrowing capacity is given utmost importance. Prudent assessment of estimates to total personal and household indebtedness as well as disposable income available for family and personal needs plus debit servicing should be shown to determine net take-home pay of the borrower which should not be less than P4,000.00 per month. Deviation if any, will require the President&rsquo;s approval.</span></li>
</ol>
