<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><strong>LOAN POLICY GUIDELINES</strong></span></div>

<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">This guideline formalizes Queen City Development Bank&rsquo;s policy and procedures in regard to the granting of <strong>&ldquo;salary-based general-purpose consumption loans&rdquo;</strong> to its existing/prospective clients. The processes outlined are minimum requirements granted under sound credit standards and fair consumer practices. Deviations are to be referred and approved in accordance with the provisions herein stated.</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">The granting of salary-based general-purpose consumption loan is not to be considered as a regular/service open or available to the general public. This refers to unsecured loans for a broad range of consumption purposes, granted to individuals mainly on the basis of regular salary, pension or other fixed compensation, where repayment would come from such future cash flows, either through salary deductions, debits from the borrower&rsquo;s deposit account, mobile payments, pay-through collections, over-the-counter payments or other type of payment arrangement agreed upon by the borrower and lender. Bank officers are urged to exercise due discretion and to follow the provisions of this Manual.</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></div>

<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">Any changes, amendments or revisions in Manual Provisions are to have approval by the Bank&rsquo;s Board of Directors.</span></div>

<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<ol>
<li><span style="font-size: 12pt;">First and foremost, you must <u>KNOW YOUR CLIENT.</u> In conducting client calls, the identity of the applicant/borrower should be ascertained, his integrity, and character evaluated. Whenever possible, background information of the applicant/borrower should be verified for its authenticity.</span></li>
</ol>
